OUR MISSION

CaptioTech is a non-profit organization founded by high schoolers from West Lafayette, Indiana. Our mission is to help better educate young students in our community by providing them with laptops and educational programs.

TECHNOLOGY

CaptioTech collects broken or old/used laptops and refurbishes them to give to kids in need of electronics.

Our goal is to provide necessary educational resources to every young student in our area, and we could really use your help. In order to continue giving laptops to kids in need, we rely on YOUR donations. If you are willing to donate old laptops or money, please email us at captiotech@gmail.com, and we can set up a donation spot. Thank you so much for your help!
